What is Dhruva?

Dhruva represents the guiding star — a symbol of constancy, determination, and brilliance. Just like the North Star that has guided travelers for centuries, Dhruva Cultural Fest celebrates talent, creativity, and the spirit of students who shine with passion and purpose.
